Your Executive Chef is Ian Summers

Ian has been with P&O Cruises since 1996 and is driven by ensuring his team deliver the best possible results, "I want them to walk tall and be proud of what they've created." Having worked on board for the past 15 years Ian loves the fact that no two days are ever the same, "it might sound like a cliche, but literally it's a new horizon and a new challenge every day." Ian's philosophy on food is that it should be "fresh, full of flavour, simply and sharply presented, and enjoyed in good company'.
