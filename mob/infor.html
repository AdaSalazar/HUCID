Environmental Policy Statement

Carnival Corporation is an international company providing cruise vacations worldwide. Our core values include preserving the marine environment and in particular the pristine condition of the waters upon which our vessels sail.
We are committed to pollution prevention, regulatory compliance and continuous improvement to our environmental management. Our Goal is to be the industry leader for environmental excellence. We will achieve this goal by continuously improving our processes to minimise environmental impact and waste. If you have any ENVIRONMENTAL concerns about the day to day running of the ship during your cruise, please bring these to the attention of a member of the Ship's Company or our Environmental Compliance Officer. Alternatively, you may contact our Environment Hotline, from a ship's telephone dial extension 30003, from a shore telephoine dial 1-305-406-5863(worldwide), 1-888-290-5105(USA) or via the website: www.carnivalcompliance.com . To contact the Vice President/Operating Line Compliance call 1-661-753-2745.
